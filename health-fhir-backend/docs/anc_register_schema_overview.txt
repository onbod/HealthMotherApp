
=====================================================
FHIR + DAK COMPLIANT ANC REGISTER SCHEMA OVERVIEW
=====================================================

CORE ENTITIES (FHIR Resources):

1. PATIENT (FHIR: Patient)
   ├── Demographics and identification
   ├── Contact information
   └── Blood group and basic health info
   └── DAK: ANC.IND01-04 (Client Information)

2. ORGANIZATION (FHIR: Organization)
   ├── Health facility information
   ├── Facility type and location
   └── DAK: ANC.IND05 (Facility Information)

3. ENCOUNTER (FHIR: Encounter)
   ├── ANC visit records
   ├── Facility and outreach visits
   └── DAK: ANC.IND06-07 (Visit Information)

CLINICAL ENTITIES:

4. PREGNANCY (FHIR: Condition)
   ├── Pregnancy history and current pregnancy
   ├── LMP, EDD, gravida, parity
   └── DAK: ANC.IND08-13 (Pregnancy Information)

5. ANC_VISIT (FHIR: Encounter)
   ├── Individual ANC visit details
   ├── Vital signs and measurements
   └── DAK: ANC.IND34-56 (Present Pregnancy)

6. OBSERVATION (FHIR: Observation)
   ├── Clinical measurements
   ├── Laboratory results
   └── DAK: ANC.IND57-75 (Clinical Data)

7. CONDITION (FHIR: Condition)
   ├── Medical conditions and risk factors
   ├── Past pregnancy complications
   └── DAK: ANC.IND14-33 (Risk Factors)

8. PROCEDURE (FHIR: Procedure)
   ├── Medical procedures and interventions
   ├── Vaccinations and screenings
   └── DAK: ANC.IND76-82 (Procedures)

9. MEDICATION_STATEMENT (FHIR: MedicationStatement)
   ├── Medications and supplements
   ├── Iron, folic acid, IPTp, etc.
   └── DAK: ANC.IND70-75 (Medications)

DELIVERY AND POSTNATAL:

10. DELIVERY (FHIR: Encounter)
    ├── Labour and delivery records
    ├── Delivery type and location
    └── DAK: ANC.IND76-82 (Delivery Information)

11. NEONATE (FHIR: Patient)
    ├── Baby information
    ├── Birth details and outcomes
    └── DAK: ANC.IND83-90 (Neonate Information)

12. POSTNATAL_CARE (FHIR: Encounter)
    ├── Postnatal care visits
    ├── Mother and baby follow-up
    └── DAK: ANC.IND91-113 (Postnatal Care)

13. DAK_INDICATORS (DAK Specific)
    ├── WHO DAK compliance tracking
    ├── Indicator completion status
    └── DAK: ANC.IND01-113 (All Indicators)

FHIR COMPLIANCE:
✅ Patient resource for client information
✅ Organization resource for facility information
✅ Encounter resource for visits and encounters
✅ Observation resource for clinical data
✅ Condition resource for medical conditions
✅ Procedure resource for medical procedures
✅ MedicationStatement resource for medications

DAK COMPLIANCE:
✅ ANC.IND01-04: Client Information
✅ ANC.IND05: Facility Information
✅ ANC.IND06-07: Visit Information
✅ ANC.IND08-13: Pregnancy Information
✅ ANC.IND14-33: Risk Factors and Past History
✅ ANC.IND34-56: Present Pregnancy (ANC Visits)
✅ ANC.IND57-75: Clinical Measurements and Medications
✅ ANC.IND76-82: Labour and Delivery
✅ ANC.IND83-90: Neonate Information
✅ ANC.IND91-113: Postnatal Care

DATA FLOW:
1. Patient registration → Patient table
2. Facility setup → Organization table
3. ANC visits → Encounter + ANC_Visit tables
4. Clinical data → Observation table
5. Medical conditions → Condition table
6. Procedures → Procedure table
7. Medications → Medication_Statement table
8. Delivery → Delivery + Neonate tables
9. Postnatal care → Postnatal_Care table
10. DAK compliance → DAK_Indicators table

This schema provides complete FHIR R4 compliance while maintaining
full WHO DAK ANC indicator tracking and reporting capabilities.
